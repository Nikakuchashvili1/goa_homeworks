About the Lunacaria Persusi

The Lunacaria persusi is a unique and friendly breed of guinea pig, known for its beautiful fur and gentle temperament. These guinea pigs make wonderful pets due to their sociable nature and adaptability to different environments.

Originating from specific regions, the Lunacaria persusi has distinct markings and a lively personality. They thrive in environments where they can interact with their owners and other pets.
